# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Personal users and students managing daily life, shared finances with a partner, academic schedules, and habits.

## Product Purpose
To provide a focused, calm environment for managing personal tasks, schedules, shared finances, and daily habits. It exists to reduce the cognitive load of everyday life coordination. Success looks like users feeling organized and relaxed rather than overwhelmed.

## Brand Personality
Calm, clean, and focused (minimalist, distraction-free). The aesthetic should feel native, soft, and approachable, with a light-mode default similar to Apple Calendar or Reminders.

## Anti-references
- No enterprise software (avoid clunky, dense, or gray data tables).
- No complex SaaS tropes (avoid excessive sidebars, noisy analytics, metric-heavy layouts).

## Design Principles
- **Calm Clarity:** Prioritize a distraction-free layout. Information should feel breathable.
- **Approachable Utility:** Softness and a native-feel over sharp, aggressive edges.
- **Essential Complexity Only:** Keep navigation simple; avoid the multi-layered sidebars typical of complex SaaS tools.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Mobile-first, large tap targets, and reduced motion are critical.
